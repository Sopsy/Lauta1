Illegal or dangerous content

We do not allow content that:

  • is illegal or promotes illegal activity.
  • contains extremely graphic or violent material.
  • incites, glorifies or organizes violence.

Additional content classified under this rule:

  • any sexual material of children, including drawn or imaginary children.
  • promoting or supporting pedophilia.

Spam

We do not allow content that:

  • contains excessive repetitive or duplicate content.
  • contains url shorteners or other link obfuscators.

Additional content classified under this rule:

  • posts solely intended to bump a thread.
  • anime, manga or other equivalent content in the board "Sekalainen".
  • telling others that you reported a post.

Malicious content

We do not allow content that:

  • contains malicious content or is linking to them.
  • promotes disrupting any website or service, including Ylilauta

Malicious content is described as:

  • phishing.
  • malware or other equivalent unwanted software.
  • videos that are meant to scare others ("screamers").

Advertising

We do not allow content that:

  • advertises any commercial or for-profit service or product.
  • contains referrer links, except where only the person clicking the link benefits from it.
  • contains links to or promotes any competitor of Ylilauta.

Harassment

We do not allow content that:

  • is intended to harass or threaten others or promotes such activity.
  • contains stalking or "doxxing".

Sexually explicit content

Except for the board "Keho ja seksuaalisuus" where it needs to be proven that it's your own original content, we do not allow content that:

  • features nudity or any form of pornography, excluding art or newsworthy material not intended to be sexually arousing in a non-sexual context when related to the topic.

Inappropriate content

Except for boards "Sekalainen", "Anime", "Bilderberg-kerhohuone" and "International", we do not allow content that:

  • is inappropriate towards others.
  • contains excessive profanity.
  • contains spoilers without [spoiler]spoiler-tags[/spoiler].
  • contains arguing over opinions, excluding threads made specifically for that purpose.
